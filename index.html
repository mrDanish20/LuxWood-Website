The Forme Atelier

Made with Hands.
Meant to Last.

FORME was founded on one conviction: that the objects you live with should be worthy of the life you're living. Every joint, finish, and proportion is deliberate. Nothing is manufactured in haste.

We partner with family-run workshops in Northern Italy, the Faroe Islands, and Kyoto — places where the knowledge of making is passed down, not just trained.
