Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take?
White Glove delivery typically takes 10-14 business days for in-stock items. Custom pieces require 8-12 weeks. You'll receive tracking updates at every stage.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 32 countries across Europe, North America, Asia, and Australia. Delivery costs are calculated at checkout based on your location.
Can I return or exchange an item?
Absolutely. We offer free 30-day returns. Our team will arrange collection and a full refund once the piece is returned in original condition.
Do you offer trade or contract pricing?
Yes, interior designers, architects, and trade professionals receive exclusive pricing. Please email trade@forme.com with your credentials.
Is it possible to customize a piece?
Many of our pieces can be customized in dimensions, wood finish, and upholstery. Our atelier team will work directly with you on bespoke commissions.
What is your warranty policy?
Every FORME piece comes with a Lifetime Structural Warranty covering all joinery, frames, and mechanisms. Upholstery and finishes are covered for 5 years.
