The Forme Atelier

Objects Made by Hand,
Meant to Last

Founded on one conviction: that the objects you live with should be worthy of the life you're living. Every joint, finish, and proportion is deliberate. Nothing is manufactured in haste.

Design Philosophy

Slowness, Honesty, Proportion

We reject the tyranny of seasonal trends. A FORME piece is designed for a lifetime — considered details that reveal themselves over years, not seconds. Every edge softened by hand, every surface chosen for its patina, not its perfection.

Our studio works closely with master carpenters, woodturners, and textile ateliers across Italy, Sweden and Japan. The result is functional sculpture that improves with age, embracing wear as narrative.

✦ slow design manifesto ✦

The Making

From Sketch to Doorstep

Every FORME piece follows an unhurried path — drawn, refined, carved, finished, and finally delivered to your home.

01
Sketch & Study
Months of proportion studies, material sampling, and hand-drawn iterations before any digital model.
02
Material Selection
FSC-certified European oak, walnut, Italian marble — each board hand-selected for grain and character.
03
Joinery & Carving
Traditional mortise-and-tenon, dovetails, and hand-chiseled details by third-generation artisans.
04
Finishing & Patina
Natural oils, waxes, and lime washes. No toxic lacquers — surfaces that breathe and age gracefully.
